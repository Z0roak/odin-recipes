Easy,Chewy Flourless Peanutbutter Cookies

Description

The total time needed for these yummy cookies is 20 minutes.

You will be able to get about 12 to 16 cookies with one recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips (optional)
  1. Preheat the oven to 175Â°C
  2. Beat peanut butter, white sugar, and brown sugar together in a lagre bowl with an electric mixer until smooth. Stir egg, vanilla extract and baking soda into peanut butter mixture; stir in chocolate chips.
  3. Drop mixture by small rounded spoonfuls onto a baking sheet about 2 inches apart.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until cookies are flattened and golden, about 8 minutes.
