Dark Chocolate Hot Cocoa Recipe

This awesome hot cocoa can be prepared in about 15 minutes total, with about 5 mins prep-time and 10 mins cooking-time.

The standart recipe yields about 1 cup although this can easily be adjusted below.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons brown sugar, or to taste
  • 2 ounces dark chocolate (such as Moser Roth's 85% Dark Chocolate), or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon heavy whipping cream, or more to taste
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon, or more to taste

Directions

Heat milk in a saucepan over medium heat until just before boiling, 3 to 4 minutes.

Add brown sugar and stir unti dissolved, about 1 minute.

Stir dark chocolate into milk until melted, 2 to 3 minutes.

Remove saucepan from heat and stir cream and cinnamon into milk mixture.

Tips

For creamier cocoa, use more heavy cream. I also like to put whipped cream at the bottom of the mug and pour the cocoa over it.
